Looper

A live stream when you just want to do some testing, not build Twitch 2.0

Keeping a live stream running is a pain. If you want to just test something or do a demo, it's totally overkill. You could waste your CPU on a local encoder or add yet-another server to restart monthly… or you could include looper.mjs in your page and let it turn any HLS stream you can find into an infinitely repeating live stream: 

<script type="module" src="looper.mjs"></script>

From the perspective of code running on your page, it's the same thing as any other live stream.
