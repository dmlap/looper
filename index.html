Looper

A live stream when you just want to do some testing, not build Twitch 2.0

Keeping a real live stream running is a huge pain. If you want to test something, demo some cool capability, or see how some interesting encoding feature works with a live stream, good luck. At minimum, you're re-reading that gripping live encoder documentation and fighting with your cloud provider of choice to spin up the flavor-of-the-month infrastructure.

…or you could include looper.mjs in your page and let it turn any VOD HLS stream you can find into an infinitely repeating live stream. Your call.
